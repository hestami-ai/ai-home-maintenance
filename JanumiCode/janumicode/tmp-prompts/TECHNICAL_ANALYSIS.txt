# Approved Implementation Plan

**Title:** Product Discovery for Hestami AI Real Property OS and Platform

**Summary:** This plan outlines the comprehensive product vision, personas, user journeys, technical architecture, and phased implementation strategy for the Hestami AI Real Property OS. This integrated platform, composed of a Home Assistant, Field Services Management, and Community Association Management pillars, will serve property owners, service providers, and community associations. The system is designed to be a secure, scalable, multi-tenant solution leveraging an AI-driven approach to streamline property management, maintenance, and governance.

## Requirements

- [REQ-1] The platform must support AI-driven extraction, classification, and summarization of documents.

- [REQ-2] The system must have an auditable, compliant GL structure for accounting.

- [REQ-3] The platform must support automatic periodic assessment posting for community associations.

- [REQ-4] The platform must provide native iOS and Android mobile clients for homeowners and technicians with offline-first capabilities.

## Constraints

- [CON-1] Cloudflare CDN will be the only public entry point to the system.

- [CON-2] External integrations with partners like Nextdoor are out-of-scope for the initial implementation.

## Decisions

- [DEC-1] The technology stack is pre-defined, including SvelteKit for the frontend, Node.js (Bun) for the backend, PostgreSQL for the database, and DBOS for the workflow engine.

- [DEC-2] The product will be built in three phases, corresponding to the three pillars: 1. Home Assistant, 2. Field Services Management, 3. Community Association Management.

- [DEC-3] Pillar 2 (Field Services Management) will be modeled after ServiceTitan.

- [DEC-4] Pillar 3 (Community Association Management) will be modeled after Vantaca.

## Validated Business Domains

User-validated business domains from INTAKE — use to inform engineering domain assessment:

- **Property & Asset Management** (DOM-PROPERTY): Core repository for all information related to a physical property, including its structure, assets, documents, and history.

- **Service & Work Order Management** (DOM-WORK-ORDER): Manages the full lifecycle of a service request, from initial creation by a homeowner to bidding, work order generation, completion, and invoicing.

- **Field Service Operations** (DOM-FIELD-OPS): Handles the operational side for service providers, including scheduling jobs, dispatching technicians, managing routes, and tracking job status in real-time.

- **Community & Compliance Management** (DOM-COMPLIANCE): Governs the rules and regulations of a community association, including inspections, violation tracking, and enforcement workflows.

- **Financials & Accounting** (DOM-ACCOUNTING): Manages all financial aspects for community associations, including accounts receivable (assessments), accounts payable, general ledger, and financial reporting.

- **AI & Data Intelligence** (DOM-AI): A cross-cutting domain that provides AI-powered services like document analysis, service provider recommendations, and predictive maintenance insights to other domains.

- **User & Identity Management** (DOM-IDENTITY): Handles user authentication, authorization, profiles, roles, and permissions across all three pillars of the platform.

- **Vendor & Provider Management** (DOM-VENDOR): Manages the network of service providers, including their onboarding, credentialing (insurance, licenses), service offerings, ratings, and performance.

- **Communications & Notifications** (DOM-COMMS): Manages all communications sent to users, including emails, SMS, push notifications, and in-app messages. Provides templates and history.

- **Reporting & Analytics** (DOM-REPORTING): Provides dashboards, reports, and data visualizations to help users like Board Members, CAMs, and Service Providers understand performance and make informed decisions.

- **Document Management** (DOM-DOCS): Provides a centralized and secure repository for storing, organizing, versioning, and sharing all documents across the platform.

- **Architectural & Change Request Management** (DOM-ARCH-REVIEW): Manages the process for homeowners to submit architectural or modification requests to the community association, and for the board/committee to review and approve them.

- **System Administration & Configuration** (DOM-ADMIN): Tools for Hestami Staff to manage the platform itself, including tenant (Community/Service Provider) onboarding, system-wide settings, feature flags, and monitoring.

## Phasing Strategy

- **Phase 1**: Focus on delivering the core value proposition to property owners with the Hestami Home Real Property Assistant.

- **Phase 2**: Build the Hestami Service Provider Field Services Management platform to onboard and serve contractors.

- **Phase 3**: Develop the Hestami Community Association Management platform to serve HOAs and property management companies.
